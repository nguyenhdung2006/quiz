How Vocabulary Quiz Works

A private, lightweight trainer with favorites, daily challenge, mistake practice, examples, notes, and mastery tracking.

1. Build The Word Bank

Add English, Vietnamese, and part of speech. The app stores everything locally in this browser.

2. Choose A Mode

Play English to Vietnamese, Vietnamese to English, or Mixed mode. Difficulty controls how many words enter the round.

3. Answer Fast

Pick one of four answers. Number keys 1-4 work too. Challenge mode adds a countdown per question.

4. Master Mistakes

Wrong answers are saved into the mistake bank. Practice them later, then clear mastered words when they are stable.

5. Review Answers

After each quiz, open the review screen to see your answer, the correct answer, examples, and notes.

6. Build Favorites

Star important words and practice them as a focused mini deck whenever you want.
